Vegan Coconut Cake with White Chocolate and Berries

Yup, vegan white chocolate! It was about time that I put some of that beautiful cacao butter to use and it worked out wonderfully. There is absolutely no need for dairy in white chocolate because it dilutes the cacao butter flavor, and not in a good way. Cacao butter is exquisite on it's own with a touch of sugar and vanilla bean.

Nothing better than a splash of fresh squeezed citrus juice

Orange season is pretty delicious right now. Some of my favorites are navel oranges, cara cara oranges, fremont tangerines, satsuma mandarines, cocktail grapefruits, and oro de blancos. I always sample fruit from the farmers markets before purchase but sometimes less desirable ones show up. So what do to do? Squeeze them!! The juice will still taste 100 times better than any store bought processed orange juice.
